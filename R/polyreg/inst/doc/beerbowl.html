The Impact of Beer Consumption on Bowling Scores

Brad Barker

Let’s reexamine the age old topic of beer and bowling. A commonly held belief is that beer offers only a minimal, then diminishing impact to a players score as drinking continues. A supporting observation of this would be a plot indicating an increase then decrease in bowling average. To achieve this, we’ll use the polyreg package and it’s included data source, beerbowl.

In our case;

  • y is the average game handicapped score by bowler
  • x is the average number of beers consumed during event over 30 frames
library(polyreg)
plot_r(beerbowl)
